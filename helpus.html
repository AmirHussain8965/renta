How can we help you?

Getting Started

1- Creating an Account

To begin using our platform, click on "Sign Up" and provide your email, password, and basic information. Complete the verification steps, and your account will be ready

2- Logging In If you already have an account, click on "Log In" and enter your email and password. You can also use social media accounts for quick access.

3- Navigating the Dashboard Upon logging in, you'll find you dashboard. Here, manage your profile, view saved rentals, and access tools for listing items or searching for rentals.

Listing Your Items

1- Adding a Rental Listing

Click "Add Listing" on your dashboard and follow the guide to enter item details, rental terms, and photos. Ensure accurate and appealing information.

2- Managing Item Details Edit your listing at any time under "My Listings." Update information, pricing, and availability as needed.

3- Uploading Photos High-quality photos attract more users. Upload clear images showcasing your item's best features. You can add multiple photos for a comprehensive view.

4- Setting Rental Terms Specify rental terms such as duration, deposit, and any additional fees. Provide clarity to avoid misunderstandings with potential renters.

5- Publishing Your Listing Once your listing is complete, click "Publish." It becomes visible to potential renters. Deactivate or delete your listing at any time.

Finding Rentals

1- Browsing Listings

Start your search by clicking "Find Rentals." Browse through various listings to discover a diverse range of items available for rent.

2- Using Search Filters Narrow down your search using filters like location, type of item, price range, and more. Simplify finding the perfect rental for your needs.

3- Saving Favourite Rentals Found an item you like? Click "Save" to add it to your favourites. Access them later under "My Favourites" on your dashboard.

4- Contacting Listers Interested in a rental? Click "Contact Lister" to send a message. Be courteous and provide relevant information about your needs.

Managing Your Account

1 Editing Your Profile

Keep your profile updated. Click "Edit Profile" to change contact information, profile picture, and other details.

2 Changing Account Settings Modify your settings by clicking "Account Settings." Update email address, password, and notification preferences.

3 Privacy and Security We prioritize your privacy and data security. Review our privacy policy to understand how we protect your information.
